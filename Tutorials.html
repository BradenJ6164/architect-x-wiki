Getting Started on SCP Architect X

New to SCP Architect X? Check out this comprehensive guide to getting started.

Creating A New Game

Everything starts with a new game save, begin by pressing the “New Game Button."

Give your foundation a name then select your gamemode, in this guide we will use “Classic." Then press, “Start New Foundation."

Understanding Game Mechanics

After creating your foundation, various buttons and information will be shown in a bar at the bottom of your screen.
