Getting Started on SCP Architect X

New to SCP Architect X? Check out this comprehensive guide to getting started.

Creating A New Game

Everything starts with a new game save, begin by pressing the “New Game Button."

Give your foundation a name then select your gamemode, in this guide we will use “Classic." Then press, “Start New Foundation."

Understanding Game Mechanics

After creating your foundation, various buttons and information will be shown in a bar at the bottom of your screen. 

These are the key components of SCP Architect X, let's learn what each one means!

  1. Level: Your foundation level. This is determined by XP earned from testing and completing tasks.
  2. Site Information: Your foundation name, money amount, and gems amount.
  3. Shop: Where you can purchase wall skins, gamepasses, etc!
  4. Foundation Management: Provides your foundation statistics such as profits and gross revenue.
  5. Foundation Staff: Allows you to hire foundation personnel and provides staff statistics such as happiness (morale).
  6. Research: This is where you advance specific aspects of your facility through research points.
     
  7. Structures: This is where wall skins can be accessed and used to build your facility. See structures.
  8. SCPs: Contains the SCPs you currently have access to place. See SCPs.
  9. Furnishings: Placeables used by your foundation staff, such as beds and toilets. See furnishings.
  10. Deletion: Use this to delete placeables.
  11. Zoning: Allows you to specify certain locations in your facility, such as laboratories and test subject cells. See zoning.
     
  12. Menu: Sends you back to the main menu.
  13. Notifications: Provides important notifications about the game and your foundation.
