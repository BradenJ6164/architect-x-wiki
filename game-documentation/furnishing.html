Furnishing

 

Furnishing is a vital tool used for placing items that can be interacted with or is a requirement for specific zones or personnel.

As of now, there are 9 available pieces of furniture that each have a specific function.

 

Kitchen Buffet

The Kitchen Buffet is used to serve your personnel and D-Class food to satisfy their hunger.

This item has a second skin, the Kitchen Counter. Regardless of which is used, they do the same task.

This item is a requirement for the Cafeteria. See Zoning for more.
