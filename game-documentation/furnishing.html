Furnishing

 

Furnishing is a vital tool used for placing items that can be interacted with or is a requirement for specific zones or personnel.

As of now, there are 9 available pieces of furniture that each have a specific function. If you are unsure what zone uses what item, check out Zoning for more.

 

Kitchen Buffet

The Kitchen Buffet is used to serve your personnel and D-Class food to satisfy their hunger.

This item has a second skin, the Kitchen Counter. Regardless of which is used, they do the same task.

This item is a requirement for the Cafeteria.

Old Bed

The old bed is used for your Class D to sleep in their cell to satisfy their sleep. This is not to be confused with the regular Bed as it can't be used by Class D.

This item has a second skin, the Old Bunk Bed. This will allow 2 Class D to accompany one cell.

This item is a requirement for the Test Subject Cell.

Toilet

The toilet is used for your personnel and Class D to use the restroom to satisfy their bathroom needs.

This item has a second skin, the Toilet Combo. Regardless of which is used, they do the same task.

This item is a requirement for the Restroom.

Bed

The bed is used for your personnel to sleep in their personal quarters to satisfy their sleep. This is not to be confused with the Old Bed as it can't be used by Personnel.
