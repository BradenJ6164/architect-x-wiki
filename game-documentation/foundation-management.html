Foundation Management

The Foundation Management menu is where you can change your facility's name, view profit margins, and manage staff.

To access Foundation Management, click the SCP Logo icon in the lower taskbar.

Home

Under home, you can change your facility's name. However, you cannot change your facility to have the same name as another save.

Profits

The profits section shows you the amount of money you make from each SCP's test and it's profit per minute/hour. This section is very useful in seeing how lucrative your facility is!

Staff

The staff section allows you to hire/fire staff, view current staff, and see their overall metrics. By keeping the bathroom, energy, and hunger metrics high, you promote faster testing and profits. This section is highly useful in optimizing your facility.
